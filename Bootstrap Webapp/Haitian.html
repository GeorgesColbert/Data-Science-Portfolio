Griot

Ingredients Amount
Boneless Pork Picnic shoulder 5-7 of lbs
Salt 1 cup
Minced Garlic 1 tbsp.
Ground Black Pepper 2 tbsp
Fresh Thyme 3-4 sprigs
1/2 Tsp. seasoned salt 1 tablespoons
oil 1 cup
Scotch Bonnet Green pepper 1 whole

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F (200 C)
  2. Place meat in a large bowl and pour 2 tbsp. of salt and Â½ cup of sour orange juice on it. Mix well and let sit for 2 minutes.
  3. Rinse meat with cold water and drain well. Place clean meat in a 6 to 8 Qt. Dutch oven or pressure cooker. Add all remaining ingredients (except oil, but including remaining 1/2 cup of sour orangr juice) and mix thoroughly. refrigerate for at least 15 minutes before cooking.
  4. Add 1 cup of water to meat and cover. Stir occasionally. Cook on Medium heat until meat is fully cooked and tender (about 40 minutes to 1 hour).
  5. Drain cooked meat, cut into medium pieces, and place in an oven safe dish.
